What is it?

Have you ever been in trouble because of something missing at home, and realizing that you don't have neither time nor a way to get to a grocery?

Then with Milkplease you can ask to get quickly the shop at home or scrape up some money making small deliveries.

Milkplease is accessible via web and mobile where you can send a request to other users in the same area, where they can deliver your shop.

How does it work?

Oops! Something missing in the fridge...

Have short time and you cannot go shopping. Let's try Milkplease!

Meanwhile at the grocery...

Someone is doing her own shop and see the request. She finds it not a big deal and she decides to take care of it.

It's just matter of time...

The shopping arrives at your door step, meanwhile Milkplease sends the money back to the delivery man with an extra compensation.
